General Question

  • HOW CAN I TRACK MY ORDER?
    • After 48 hours of ordering, we'll send you a tracking id on your WhatsApp.The tracking number keeps you updated on the status of your delivery.
  • CAN I CHANGE MY SHIPPING ADDRESS AFTER PAYMENT?
      • •Send us a message and we'll work it out with our courier partner.

        •If your package hasn't been picked up yet, we can definitely change your shipping address.

        •But once the package has been picked up by our courier, do take note that it will take a while to reroute it. To avoid any trouble, double-check your delivery address before confirming your order.
  • CAN YOU DO SOMETHING FOR A SPEEDY DELIVERY?
    • Yes, you can make full payment using the prepaid method and request our salesperson to deliver it faster.
  • WHAT STEPS CAN I TAKE IF THE PRODUCT I RECEIVE IS WRONG OR DAMAGED PRODUCT?
    • You can contact our customer care department and they will resolve your issue within 24 hours (+91 96346 11069).
  • CAN I RECEIVE DELIVERY OF YOUR PRODUCTS OUTSIDE THE COUNTRY?
    • We're sorry, but we are currently only shipping within India. We are unable to provide delivery outside the country at this time.
  • CHAT SUPPORT?
    • Yes, We provide whatsapp chat support.
  • CAN YOU PROVIDE SAMPLES?
    • Currently, We are not providing samples.
  • DO YOU PROVIDE C.O.D.?
    • Yes, We Provide Cash On Delivery, but you have to pay 50 Extra.
  • CAN YOU PROVIDE DETAILS ABOUT THE NOTES UTILIZED IN YOUR PERFUMES?
    • Yes, before purchasing your order our salesperson will guide you with your doubts regarding notes, longevity, projection, and silage, otherwise you can check them on the product's description as well.
