About Us

Welcome to Royal Bloomaa – A Scent That Speaks Volumes.

At Royal Bloomaa, we believe that luxury fragrances should be within everyone's reach. Inspired by the rich traditions of Kannauj, the perfume capital of India, we craft high-quality perfumes and attars that blend
heritage with modern innovation. Our mission is to create exquisite, long-lasting scents that leave a lasting impression—without the luxury price tag.

With a deep passion for perfumery, we have recreated over 700+ fragrances, capturing the essence of timeless scents while making them accessible to all.

Our Mission & Vision

Our goal is simple—to bring premium yet affordable perfumes to every corner of India and beyond. We are committed to making luxury fragrances accessible, so everyone can experience the magic of fine perfumery.

What Makes Us Unique?

✔️ Long-Lasting Scents: 25% perfume concentration ensures 8+ hours of longevity for a captivating fragrance experience.

✔️ Authentic Ingredients: We use the finest essential oils from Kannauj, a region renowned for its expertise in perfumery.

✔️ Affordable Luxury: Breaking the myth that high-quality perfumes must be expensive, we offer superior quality at an accessible price.

✔️ Extensive Collection: With 700+ recreated fragrances, we bring you a diverse range of scents inspired by
the world’s most iconic perfumes.

Our Promise

At Royal Bloomaa, we are passionate about blending tradition with modern craftsmanship to create fragrances that evoke emotions, confidence, and timeless elegance.

Join us in redefining affordable luxury and discover a scent that truly represents you.

Explore our collection today and experience the magic of Royal Bloomaa!

Our History

10/2023

2019 – The Vision Begins Inspired by the rich perfume heritage of Kannauj, our founder envisioned a brand that would bring high-quality, long-lasting perfumes to everyone at affordable prices. The idea of Royal Bloomaa was born.

07/2024

2021 – Crafting the Perfect Formula After years of research and testing, we perfected our formula with 25% concentration, ensuring 8+ hours of longevity. We sourced the finest essential oils from Kannauj, blending tradition with modern craftsmanship.

To be Continue....

08/2024

2023 – Launch of Royal Bloomaa With a strong passion for perfumery, Royal Bloomaa officially launched, offering luxury fragrances at accessible prices. Our perfumes quickly gained popularity among fragrance lovers across India.

01/2025

2025 – Expanding Beyond Borders With growing demand and love from our customers, we are now on a mission to expand Royal Bloomaa beyond India, bringing our affordable luxury perfumes to the global market.
